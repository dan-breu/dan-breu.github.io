Hey, I'm Dan

I'm a founder, security engineer, and builder based in Vancouver. I work at the intersection of software development, application security, and entrepreneurship — currently building at ekiscyber.

This site is where I share writing, projects, and resources around AppSec, DevSec, productivity, and building software products.

What I do

  • AppSec & DevSec — securing modern applications and development pipelines
  • Building products — from idea to shipped software
  • Writing & teaching — notes, guides, and reflections on the craft

Currently

Founder at @ekiscyber. CISSP certified. Passionate about zero-trust architecture, developer tooling, and macOS productivity.
